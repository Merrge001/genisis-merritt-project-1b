About

Genesis is a vibrant and talented 20 year old solo singer,songwriter, manager and producer from Cleveland,Ohio whose focus genre is in R&B and Soul, but also occasionally does Gospel and Hip Hop as well. Growing up in church and being heavily involved in the choir, Genesis has always had a love for music and has recently really developed a passion for it again since the pandemic. Her hobbies include: traveling, drawing or painting, and hanging out with her friends. She has currently released one song called, “Stay Right” ft. Messenger, and produced “Show Me” by Keir Eleaine, but plans on releasing many more in the future. Some of her major influences include Sza, Jhene Aiko, Summer Walker, Ari Lennox, Aretha Franklin, Jennifer Hudson and Beyoncé. As an up and coming artist, Genesis 1:1 wishes to give the people something they can feel not only in their hearts, but also their souls.
